FAQ

Solar energy is a renewable energy source that comes from the sun. It can be harnessed through solar panels to generate electricity for homes and businesses.

Solar panels are designed to last for 25-30 years or more. They require minimal maintenance and have no moving parts, which makes them highly durable.

he amount of energy that solar panels can produce depends on several factors, such as the size of the system, the location, and the weather conditions. At Solight Energy, we can help you determine the optimal size and placement of your solar panel system to maximize energy production.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance and can self-clean during rain showers. However, if you live in a dusty or dirty area, you may need to clean your solar panels periodically to ensure optimal energy production.
